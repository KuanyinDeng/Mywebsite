About the Embassy

The Embassy of the Republic of South Sudan in Cairo serves as the official diplomatic mission representing the interests of South Sudan and its citizens in Egypt. Our mission is to strengthen bilateral relations, provide consular services, and offer assistance to South Sudanese nationals residing in or visiting Egypt.

Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure the welfare of our citizens and to promote the rich cultural heritage of South Sudan.
